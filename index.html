About Us

Lenatech Robotics & STEM Academy is an initiative under Lenatech Finance focused on expanding access to practical STEM education across Kenya, with a special focus on underserved ASAL communities. We deliver hands-on robotics and coding programs, AI literacy sessions, and teacher capacity-building designed to work in low-resource environments.

Our Approach

  • Hands-on project learning using low-cost robotics kits and accessible digital tools.
  • Mentor-led workshops, school STEM club support and community outreach.
  • Teacher training so schools and youth centers can sustain programs independently.

Program Impact (first-year targets)

Reach 500 learners across Nairobi and surrounding counties, train 50 teachers, and establish 20 school STEM clubs — with priority support for learners from Samburu and other ASAL regions.

Programs & Services

Robotics & Coding Bootcamp

12-week cohorts teaching block & Python coding, sensors, motors, and simple automation projects tailored for low-resource environments.

STEM Clubs

Setup and sustain STEM clubs in partner schools with curriculum guides, mentor training, and competition-readiness.

AI Literacy Workshops

Introductory AI concepts, ethics, and simple ML demonstrations using accessible tools and examples relevant to local challenges.

Teacher & Leader Training

Short training sessions enabling teachers and youth leaders to run basic robotics and coding lessons without prior expertise, including lesson plans and low-cost project guides.

Upcoming Cohort & ASAL Outreach

Our upcoming 12-week cohort is designed to include learners from Nairobi as well as Samburu and neighboring ASAL counties. We run in-person hubs (Nairobi + partner community centers) and provide remote mentorship, learning packs and scholarship support to allow ASAL learners to participate fully.

ASAL Outreach Program

Targeted activities in Samburu, Marsabit, Isiolo, Laikipia North, Turkana and Baringo to build local STEM capacity, train community champions, and supply learning materials.

Scholarships & Support

We provide partial and full scholarships for learners from low-income households in ASAL regions; transport and remote learning support are available for selected participants.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower young Africans — especially those from ASAL and rural regions — with practical Robotics, AI and STEM skills that prepare them for future opportunities in technology and innovation.

Vision: A future where every young person—regardless of background—has access to quality STEM education and the chance to build solutions for their communities.
