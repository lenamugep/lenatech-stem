About Us

Lenatech STEM & Robotics Program is an initiative under Lenatech Finance, dedicated to expanding access to digital skills, coding, AI literacy, and introductory robotics education for young people in Kenya. Our mission is to empower youth—especially those from underserved communities—with the skills needed to participate in the future of work and technology-driven innovation.

Programs We Offer

  • Computer Literacy & Digital Skills Training
  • Python Programming for Beginners
  • AI & Machine Learning Basics
  • STEM Clubs Support & Youth Leadership Training
  • Introduction to Robotics & Automation Concepts
