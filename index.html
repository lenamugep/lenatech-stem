About Us

Lenatech Robotics & STEM Academy is an initiative under Lenatech Finance focused on expanding access to practical STEM education in Kenya. We run hands-on robotics and coding programs, AI literacy sessions, and capacity building for teachers and youth leaders. Our methods are project-based, affordable, and inclusive—designed to work in low-resource environments.

Our Approach

  • Hands-on project learning using low-cost robotics kits and digital tools.
  • Mentor-led workshops and school STEM club support.
  • Teacher training so schools can sustain programs independently.

Program Impact (target)

In our first year we aim to reach 500 learners across Nairobi and surrounding counties, train 50 teachers, and establish 20 school STEM clubs.

Programs & Services

Robotics & Coding Bootcamp

12-week cohorts teaching block & Python coding, sensors, motors, and simple automation projects.

STEM Clubs

Setup and sustain STEM clubs in partner schools with curriculum guides and mentor training.

AI Literacy Workshops

Introductory AI concepts, ethics, and simple ML demonstrations using accessible tools.

Teacher & Leader Training

Short training sessions that enable teachers and youth leaders to run basic robotics and coding lessons without prior expertise.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower young Africans with practical Robotics, AI and STEM skills that prepare them for future careers in technology and innovation.

Vision: A future where every young person—regardless of background—has access to quality STEM education and the chance to build solutions for their communities.
